Our Story

About Zero Starvation

We believe in a world where no one goes hungry while perfectly good food goes to waste. Our mission is to create sustainable connections between food surplus and food scarcity.

Our Mission

To eliminate food waste and hunger by creating an efficient, technology-driven platform that connects food donors with organizations serving vulnerable communities. We strive to build a sustainable ecosystem where excess food becomes a resource for hope.

Our Vision

A world where food security is guaranteed for all, where communities work together to ensure that nutritious food reaches every person in need, and where food waste becomes a thing of the past through collaborative action and shared responsibility.

The Challenge

The Problem We're Solving

Food Waste Crisis

India wastes 67 million tonnes of food annually — enough to feed the entire state of Bihar for a year. Restaurants alone contribute significantly, with perfectly edible food ending up in landfills daily.

Hunger Crisis

Over 194 million people in India are undernourished, including millions of children who go to bed hungry every night. Many NGOs struggle to find consistent, reliable sources of food.

Our Solution

Zero Starvation creates a direct bridge between these two problems, turning food waste into food security through technology, community partnerships, and shared compassion.
